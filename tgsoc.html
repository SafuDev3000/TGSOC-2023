Welcome to The Grand Scheme of Crypto

In 2021, amid the challenges of life and a life-altering car accident, the inspiration for our Crypto Media and Consultancy/SaaS Company was ignited. While juggling the responsibilities of a DoorDash driver and immersing myself in the complex worlds of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology, I made a profound realization - the cryptocurrency landscape operates at a breathtaking pace, and staying informed is absolutely crucial

It became evident that there was a significant void to be filled in this fast-paced industry, where missing a single day could mean forfeiting a month's worth of innovation and opportunities. And so, our journey began – the birth of our platform, dedicated to disseminating the latest and most impactful technological advancements in the realms of the internet and blockchain.

We embarked on a mission to become the missing voice, addressing the uncharted yet revolutionary developments in this rapidly evolving sector. Our commitment is to deliver dynamic and insightful media content while offering top-tier consultancy and SaaS solutions, ensuring that individuals and businesses remain ahead of the curve in the ever-changing world of crypto.
