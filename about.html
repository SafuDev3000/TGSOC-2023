Our Journey in the World of Crypto

The Story Behind The Grand Scheme Of Crypto

It all began in 2021, amidst the trials of life and a severe car accident, when the vision for this Youtube Channel emerged. Balancing work as a DoorDash driver and delving deep into the realms of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology, I realized something profound - the world of crypto moves at lightning speed, and staying abreast is vital.

I saw an underserved need for more dynamic and insightful media outlets in this fast-paced industry. Missing a single day in crypto can feel like losing out on a month's worth of innovation and opportunities. Thus, our channel was born - a platform dedicated to broadcasting the latest and most significant technological innovations in the internet and blockchain spaces. We set out to be the voice that wasn't there, covering underrepresented yet groundbreaking developments in this rapidly evolving sector.

The Odyssey of Geno Grand

Meet Geno Grand, a seasoned Web3 participant, investor, and entrepreneur, whose journey through the digital realm is nothing short of extraordinary. It all started during the nascent days of Instagram, when social media was just beginning to reshape our world.

Geno's adventure took a pivotal turn with the rise of Twitter and the explosive launch of NFTs, pioneered by artists like Beeple and Latasha.eth. It was a time of exciting beginnings and groundbreaking developments in the blockchain world.

Before the glitz of the digital age, there were humbler days. Geno reminisces about his trips to local grocery stores, meeting up with online acquaintances to exchange crypto peer-to-peer - a time when each transaction was a leap of faith and a step towards an unforeseen future.

Interestingly, Geno's roots trace back to the infancy of the cybersecurity industry, where he delved into the complexities of SQL injection and other emerging threats. His crypto journey, characterized by modest beginnings from mere $10 to $100, mirrors the experiences of many in this space. It's a story not of vast wealth, but of resilience, learning, and growth.

Geno's path is a testament to the transformative power of technology and a source of inspiration for those embarking on their own crypto journey. It's a journey of innovation, opportunity, and the relentless pursuit of progress.
